My journey began with the WinterSports Company


In November 2019, I seized the moment to do something I've always wanted to do, and I booked myself into an intership with The WinterSports Company to become a ski instructor in Canada. I had always wanted to work for a ski season and figured I needed a career break, so I would go away for a few months and come back home. Instead this new job, my passion, had become so much more than I ever imagined!

Through 2 seasons of teaching, I still feel blessed to be able to work in place like this...


I always center my lesson around creating a comfortable environment where my clients can feel at ease and gradually build confidence.

Come with me to see the town from above, I promise it will be a view you'll never forget!

Confidence in your skiing can be a mental game, and sometimes knowing you have the right eqipment is half of the battle.



Wanting to expand my knowledge, I also work part-time in one of our local ski shops, Jardines.

This has given me a deeper understanding of all the equipment we use whilst skiing, allowing me in turn to pass that knowledge on to my clients and give you the confidence you're in safe hands.
